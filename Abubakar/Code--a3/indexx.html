The Forty Rules of Love

The Forty Rules of Love by Elif Shafak intertwines the modern-day story of a housewife with the spiritual bond between Rumi and Shams of Tabriz. Through forty timeless rules, the novel explores divine love, inner transformation, and the essence of spiritual awakening.

Rich Dad Poor Dad

Rich Dad Poor Dad by Robert Kiyosaki challenges traditional views on money and financial education by contrasting lessons from his “poor dad” (own father) with those from his “rich dad” (his friend’s father).

Think Like a Monk

Think Like a Monk by Jay Shetty offers practical wisdom drawn from ancient monastic principles to help readers overcome negativity, find clarity, and live a more meaningful life. It blends mindfulness, self-discipline, and personal growth into everyday tools for mental well-being.

Sapiens

Sapiens by Yuval Noah Harari takes readers on a fascinating journey through the evolution of Homo sapiens, exploring how biology, history, and culture shaped modern civilization.
